Ingredients

  • Medium sized saucepan
  • Pasta pot
  • Fresh fettucine
  • Chopped parsley
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Milk
  • Cream
  • Ground pepper and Kosher salt

How to make the sauce

  1. Get a medium sized saucepan and pour about 5 cups of milk
  2. Put the pan on the stove and the milk simmer
  3. Immediately pour the carton of cream into the pan, and stir
  4. Add your desired amount of grated cheese into the pan
  5. Turn up the stove to a medium level
  6. Keep stirring as the cheese melts
  7. Once it melts, turn of the stove and start making the pasta
